About Us

SYNC Agency began in 2022 as a talent management company, dedicated to nurturing emerging creators with tailored support and growth strategies.

SYNC now connects brands with the heart of music—whether it's Afrobeats, Rap, Pop, or beyond. With a strong London-based dance community and an expanding roster of diverse creators, we deliver high-impact content that resonates with today’s culture.
